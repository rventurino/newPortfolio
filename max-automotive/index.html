Welcome to Max Automotive Repair

Max Automtive repair provides auto repair services you can count on!

Max Automotive Repair has been providing customers in the Pittsburgh, PA 15203 area with the best quality auotomtive repair and maintenance since 1984. Our experienced technicians will keep your car running perfectly, or fix any problems you may have along your travels.

Max Automotive provides a range of services including Brakes, Domestic Cars & Trucks, Heating and Cooling, Oil Change services, Engine maintenance, and 4x4 services. We are incredibly proud of our skilled mechanics, many of whom have been with us for over 20 years. We have the knowledge and expertise to diagnose your problems, while providing you affordable solutions to get you back up and running ASAP.

Feel free to call us at (202) 555-5309 or email us today. We look forward to building a lasting relationship with you for all of your maintenance, repair, and diagnostic needs.

Brake Serivces

Your car's brake pads are one of the most important safety features. Max Automotive Repair wants you to be safe, so stop in any time and we will check your brakes free of charge. Brake services available:

  • Anti-lock brake services
  • Brake Fluid Flushes
  • Brake pad and shoe replacement
  • Brake hoses
  • Calipers
  • Rotor resurfacing


30/60/90K Mileage Maintenance

As time passes, various components start to wear and fluids begin to degrade. This is where Max Automotive comes in. Maintenance packages include:

  • Fluid Replacements
  • Oil and filter changes
  • Tire rotation, spin balance, and pressure adjustments
  • Coolant flush
  • For higher mileage vehicles:
    • Spark Plugs
    • Timing belts
    • Cam Shaft adjustments


Electrical Sercices

You may have never known that your car contains complex electrical systems including it's own little generator called the alternator. Max Automotive's specialists know exactly what to do when your electrical system is giving you trouble. We can help with:

  • Batteries
  • Alternators
  • Starters
  • Diagnostics


Engine Services

Maintaining your engine is absolutely essential for keeping your car or truck running at peak performance. Not only does engine maintenace help your car run better, it helps it to run longer. Regular services such as these are important parts of a healthy car's life:

  • Fuel Injectors
  • Ignition System
  • Oil, lube, and filter
  • Spark plugs
  • Timing belts
  • Last but not least; we will diagnose the issue behind that dreadful check engine light




Tires Services

Tires are the most important part of keeping your car safe. They're the only part of your vehicle that makes contact with the road and they work in conjunction with your braking system to safely bring you to a stop. Nothing lasts forever, and that includes tires, so bring your vehicle in and we can make sure you are riding safe. We offer the following tire services:

  • Alignments
  • Balancing
  • Repairs
  • We carry all major brands of tires

    • Hankook
    • And many more...
